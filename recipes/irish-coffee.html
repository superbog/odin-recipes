Irish Coffee

Description:

Irish coffee is a hot caffeinated cocktail consisting of coffee, Irish cream liqueur, and Irish whiskey. It's topped with whipped cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish cream liqueur
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish whiskey
  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon whipped cream
  • 1 dash ground nutmeg

Steps :

  1. Combine Irish cream liqueur and Irish whiskey in a glass mug; pour in hot brewed coffee. Top with whipped cream and a dash of nutmeg.
